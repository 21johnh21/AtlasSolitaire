Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Atlas Solitaire?

Atlas Solitaire follows classic Klondike solitaire rules with a geographic twist. Instead of matching traditional suits (hearts, diamonds, clubs, spades), you match cards from the same geographic group (countries, continents, regions).

  • Draw cards from the stock pile
  • Build descending sequences in the tableau
  • Complete foundation piles from Ace to King
  • Match cards from the same geographic group

What makes Atlas Solitaire different from regular solitaire?

Instead of the same four suits every game, Atlas Solitaire features rotating geographic themes. One game might have Asian countries, European countries, African countries, and American countries. The next game could feature different groupings like continents, regions, or other geographic categories.

How do Game Center leaderboards work?

Connect to Game Center to compete globally on two leaderboards:

  • Fastest Time: Complete a game in the shortest time
  • Fewest Moves: Win with the minimum number of moves

You'll also unlock achievements as you progress!

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Toggle "Ads" off. You can re-enable them anytime.

Does the game work offline?

Yes! Atlas Solitaire works perfectly offline. You only need an internet connection for Game Center features and ads (if enabled).

Can I resume a game later?

Absolutely! Your game automatically saves. Exit anytime and pick up right where you left off from the main menu.

What devices are supported?

Atlas Solitaire runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS 26.0 or later. It supports both portrait and landscape orientations.

Is Atlas Solitaire educational?

Yes! While playing, you'll naturally learn about countries, continents, and geographic regions. It's perfect for students, geography enthusiasts, and anyone curious about the world.

How do I enable haptic feedback or sound?

Go to Settings from the main menu. You can toggle sound effects and haptic feedback on or off according to your preference.

Having Technical Issues?

The app is crashing or freezing

Try these steps:

  1. Force quit the app and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
  4. Check that you have iOS 26.0 or later
  5. If issues persist, contact support below

Game Center won't connect

Make sure:

  • You're signed in to Game Center in iOS Settings
  • You have an internet connection
  • Game Center is enabled for Atlas Solitaire in iOS Settings ‚Üí Game Center
