Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Quick Summary: Atlas Solitaire is designed with your privacy in mind. We don't collect, store, or sell your personal information. The game uses contextual ads based on geography (not personal tracking), and Game Center data is handled by Apple. You can disable ads at any time.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Atlas Solitaire ("we," "our," or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We DON'T Collect

Atlas Solitaire is designed to respect your privacy:

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
  • No Location Tracking: We don't access or store your location
  • No Device Identifiers: We don't collect or store unique device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • No Browsing History: We don't track your web browsing or app usage outside of Atlas Solitaire
  • No Photo or Camera Access: We don't access your photos, camera, or media library
  • No Contacts Access: We don't access your contact list

3. Data That Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game Progress: Your current game state, allowing you to resume games
  • Statistics: Win count, win streak, best streak (stored locally only)
  • Settings: Your preferences for sound, haptics, and ads
  • Game History: Recent deck combinations to ensure variety

Important: This data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all this data is permanently removed.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

Atlas Solitaire displays ads through Google AdMob. Here's how ads work in our app:

  • Contextual Ads Only: Ads are based on geography and game category (card games), NOT personal tracking
  • No Cross-App Tracking: We've disabled personalized ads that track you across apps
  • Privacy-First Approach: We use Google's privacy-safe ad delivery
  • User Control: You can disable ads completely in Settings

What AdMob May Collect (handled by Google):

  • General location (country/city level only, for relevant ads)
  • Device type and OS version
  • App performance metrics

AdMob's Privacy Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

4.2 Apple Game Center

If you choose to use Game Center features (leaderboards, achievements):

  • Optional: Game Center is completely optional
  • Apple's Service: All Game Center data is managed by Apple, not us
  • What's Shared: Your Game Center nickname, scores, and achievements
  • Control: Managed through your iOS Settings → Game Center

Apple's Game Center Privacy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, there's nothing to use! The only data handling happens for:

  • Local Game State: Saving your progress on your device
  • Ad Delivery: Showing contextual ads (geography-based, no personal tracking)
  • Game Center: Optional leaderboards and achievements (managed by Apple)

6. Data Sharing

We don't share any data because we don't collect any personal data to share.

The only "sharing" that occurs:

  • Google AdMob: Anonymous ad performance metrics (handled automatically by their SDK)
  • Apple Game Center: Optional scores/achievements you choose to submit

7. Children's Privacy

Atlas Solitaire is safe for all ages, including children:

  • We don't knowingly collect data from children
  • No personal information is collected from anyone
  • Educational content (geography) is appropriate for all ages
  • Ads can be disabled in Settings
  • Complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

8. Your Choices and Controls

You have complete control over your experience:

  • Disable Ads: Settings → Toggle "Ads" off
  • Disable Sound: Settings → Toggle "Sound" off
  • Disable Haptics: Settings → Toggle "Haptics" off
  • Game Center: iOS Settings → Game Center → Enable/Disable
  • Delete All Data: Delete the app from your device

9. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • No personal data is transmitted over the network
  • Local data is stored using iOS's secure storage mechanisms
  • No user accounts or passwords required
  • All third-party services (AdMob, Game Center) use industry-standard security

10. International Users

Atlas Solitaire is available worldwide. Since we don't collect personal data, there are no data transfer concerns. AdMob and Game Center handle their own regional compliance (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Post the new policy on this page
  • If changes are significant, we'll notify you in the app

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

  • Email: geo.gnosis.dev@gmail.com
  • Support: Atlas Solitaire Support

13. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Know: What data we collect (none)
  • Right to Delete: Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Disable ads in Settings
  • Right to Access: All data is stored locally on your device

14. Legal Compliance

Atlas Solitaire complies with:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California)
  • COPPA (Children's privacy)
  • Apple's App Store Guidelines
  • Google's AdMob Policies

Summary

The Bottom Line:

  • ✅ We don't collect personal information
  • ✅ Game data stays on your device
  • ✅ Ads are contextual (geography-based), not tracking-based
  • ✅ You can disable ads anytime
  • ✅ Game Center is optional and managed by Apple
  • ✅ Safe for all ages, including children
  • ✅ Compliant with privacy laws worldwide
